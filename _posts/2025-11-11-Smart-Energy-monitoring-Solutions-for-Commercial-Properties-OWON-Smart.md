## **Frequently Asked Questions: Professional Energy Monitoring**

### **Q: What accuracy level is required for tenant billing applications?**

For submetering applications where billing disputes may occur, ±2% accuracy is typically required, though many jurisdictions mandate ±1% for billing purposes. OWON Smart's meters achieve ±1% accuracy across their specified operating range, ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements.

### **Q: How does Zigbee compare to Wi-Fi for large property deployments?**

Zigbee's mesh networking capability typically supports 50-100 devices per gateway in commercial environments, making it ideal for dense deployments. Wi-Fi solutions work best where network infrastructure already exists and device counts are lower. We help clients select the optimal technology based on property size and existing infrastructure.
