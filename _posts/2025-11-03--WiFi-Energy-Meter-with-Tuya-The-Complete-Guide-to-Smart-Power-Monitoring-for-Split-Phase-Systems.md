**Understanding Split-Phase Power Monitoring Challenges**

Split-phase electrical systems, typically found in residential and light commercial applications across North America, present specific monitoring requirements that single-phase or three-phase solutions cannot adequately address. Common pain points include:

* Inability to monitor both 120V branch circuits and 240V appliance circuits simultaneously
* Limited circuit-level visibility leading to unexplained energy spikes
* Complex installation processes requiring extensive electrical work
* Lack of integration capabilities with existing building management systems
* Difficulty allocating energy costs across departments or tenants accurately
